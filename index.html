Why This Site Matters

Native plants are essential to the health of New York State’s ecosystems. They support pollinators, improve soil health, and help maintain biodiversity. This site provides clear, accessible information to help you understand and support native plant conservation in your everyday life.

Why Native Plants Are Important

Native plants have evolved alongside local wildlife, making them critical for pollinators like bees and butterflies. They require less maintenance, use less water, and are more resilient to local climate conditions compared to non-native species.
